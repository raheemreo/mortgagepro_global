⚠️ Key Risks to Consider
• Equity loan repayment is % of property value — if prices rise, you repay more.
• Fees escalate rapidly after year 5 (RPI + 1% p.a.).
• You cannot part-repay less than 10% of property value.
• Scheme closed Oct 2022 — existing borrowers only.
