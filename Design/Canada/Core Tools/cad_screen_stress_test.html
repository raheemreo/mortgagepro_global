ℹ️ About the Mortgage Stress Test
The OSFI B-20 stress test requires all federally regulated lenders to qualify borrowers at the higher of: their contract rate + 2%, or 7.00% (the current regulatory minimum as of June 2026). This applies to both insured and uninsured mortgages.
