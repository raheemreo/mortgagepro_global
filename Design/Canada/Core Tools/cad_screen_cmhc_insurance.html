⚠️ Ontario & Quebec PST on CMHC
Ontario adds 8% PST and Quebec adds 9% QST on the CMHC premium — this must be paid upfront and cannot be rolled into the mortgage. Other provinces have no additional tax.
✅ CMHC Insurance Eligibility Rules
Purchase price must be under CA$1,500,000 (effective Dec 15, 2024)
Minimum 5% down for homes under CA$500K
5% on first CA$500K + 10% on remainder up to CA$999,999
Amortization max 25 years (30 yr for first-time buyers, new builds)
Property must be owner-occupied in Canada
