Property Acquisition Rules RBI Guide →
🏠
Residential Property
Purchase / Sale by NRI/PIO/OCI
✓ Allowed
NRI/PIO/OCI can buy any number of residential properties in India.
No prior RBI approval needed — general permission under FEMA.
Payment must be made in INR via normal banking channels (NRE/NRO/FCNR account).
Foreign currency cheques / DD from abroad not allowed.
Agricultural land, plantation land, and farmhouses cannot be purchased by NRIs without RBI approval.
Gift or inheritance of agricultural land is permitted.
🏢
Commercial Property
Office, shop, godown acquisition
✓ Allowed
NRI/PIO/OCI can freely acquire commercial immovable property in India.
Includes shops, offices, industrial units.
Repatriation of rent income abroad requires an Authorised Dealer (AD) bank certification.
Net of applicable Indian income tax (TDS at 30%).

NRI Home Loan Process Step Guide →
📄
Step 1 · Documents
Submit KYC + Income Proof
Passport, Visa, NRE/NRO statement, 6-month foreign salary slips, overseas employment letter, PAN card.
🏦
Step 2 · Eligibility
Bank Assesses Loan-to-Value
NRI loans: max 80% LTV for ≤₹75L, 75% for ₹75L–₹5Cr. CIBIL score ≥700 required. Tenure max 30 years.
✅
Step 3 · Sanction
Sanction Letter Issued
Valid 3–6 months. Interest rate linked to RLLR (Repo Rate + Spread). Power of Attorney notarized.
🔑
Step 4 · Disbursement
Funds Released in INR Only
Credited directly to seller/builder. FEMA mandates INR disbursement via AD bank channel.
💰
Step 5 · Repayment
NRE/NRO Auto-Debit EMI
EMI via inward remittance, NRE/NRO debit, or rental income from property (Section 24b applies).
