Documents Required
📄
Identity & Address Proof
Aadhaar Card (mandatory), PAN Card, Voter ID or Passport. Address proof must match Aadhaar.
💰
Income Certificate
Salary slips (3 months), Form 16, or ITR for salaried. For self-employed: CA-certified income declaration.
🏠
Property Documents
Sale agreement, allotment letter, NOC from builder. Property must be in applicant's name (women preferred for EWS/LIG).
🏦
Bank Statements
Last 6 months bank statement. No existing housing property owned by any family member in India.
