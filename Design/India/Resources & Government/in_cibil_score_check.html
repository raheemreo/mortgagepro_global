💡 RBI-backed tips to boost your CIBIL
1
Pay all EMIs & credit card dues on time. Payment history accounts for 35% of your score. Even one missed payment drops score by 50–100 pts.
2
Keep credit utilisation below 30%. Using ₹30,000 on a ₹1L limit is ideal. High utilisation signals financial stress to bureaus.
3
Avoid multiple hard enquiries. Each loan/card application triggers a hard pull. Multiple applications in 90 days reduce score significantly.
4
Dispute errors on your CIBIL report. 1 in 5 Indians has an error. File a dispute at cibil.com — errors must be resolved in 30 days per RBI mandate.
5
Maintain a mix of credit types. Home loan + credit card + personal loan mix signals responsible credit behaviour to lenders.
