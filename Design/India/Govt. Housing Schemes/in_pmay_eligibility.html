How to Apply — PMAY Urban 2.0
1
Check Eligibility
Visit pmaymis.gov.in · Enter Aadhaar number · Check beneficiary status
2
Apply Through Bank or ULB
Apply via PLI (Primary Lending Institution — any PMAY-listed bank) or Urban Local Body (ULB/municipality)
3
Submit Documents
Aadhaar · Income proof · Address proof · Property documents · Bank account details
4
Subsidy Credited to Loan
NHB/HUDCO verifies and credits CLSS subsidy directly to loan account, reducing outstanding principal
5
Track Application
Track status on pmaymis.gov.in using Aadhaar number or application ID
