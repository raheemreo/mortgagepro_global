Key LAP Facts — India 2025
📌 Important LAP Guidelines (RBI / India)
Residential properties: max LTV 60% as per RBI norms. Commercial: up to 70%.
LAP rates currently range 9.25% – 12% p.a. depending on credit score and property type.
Maximum tenure: 15 years (most lenders). Loan amount: ₹10 Lakh to ₹10 Crore.
CIBIL score ≥ 700 required. 750+ gets you the best rates.
Interest on LAP used for business is tax-deductible under Section 37(1) of Income Tax Act.
Processing fee is typically 0.5%–1.5% of loan amount (+ GST @ 18%).
