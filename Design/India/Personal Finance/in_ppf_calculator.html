PPF Rules & Features
📅
Loan facility from 3rd–6th year
Borrow up to 25% of balance at 2% above PPF rate. Repay within 36 months.
💸
Partial withdrawal from 7th year
Withdraw up to 50% of balance at end of 4th year. Only once per financial year.
⏰
Extension in 5-year blocks
After 15 years, extend without contribution (just interest) or with fresh contributions (80C eligible).
🏦
Account transfer allowed
Transfer between Post Offices and scheduled banks (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, etc.) anytime.

Minor account allowed
Open in minor's name — guardian manages until age 18. Counts towards guardian's 80C limit.
