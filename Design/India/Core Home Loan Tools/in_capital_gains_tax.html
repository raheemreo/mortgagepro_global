Capital Gains Exemptions – Save Your Tax
🏠
Section 54 – Reinvest in Residential Property
Full LTCG
Exempt LTCG by purchasing one new residential property in India within 1 year before or 2 years after sale, or constructing within 3 years. LTCG amount must be invested, not the sale price.
LTCG only 1 property max (2 allowed once if gain ≤₹2Cr) 3-yr lock-in on new property
💰
Section 54F – Reinvest Full Sale Proceeds
Proportional
For non-residential asset sales. Invest entire sale consideration (not just gain) in one residential property within 2 years. Proportional exemption if partial investment. Can't own more than 1 house at time of sale.
All assets except residential property Entire sale price must be invested
📜
Section 54EC – NHAI / REC Bonds
₹50L max
Invest LTCG (up to ₹50 Lakh) in NHAI or REC bonds within 6 months of property sale. Bonds have 5-year lock-in. 5.25% interest (taxable). No TDS but interest must be declared in ITR.
NHAI / REC / PFC / IRFC bonds Within 6 months ₹50L limit
🌾
Section 54B – Agricultural Land
Full LTCG
LTCG from sale of agricultural land can be exempt if proceeds are reinvested in agricultural land within 2 years of sale. Land must have been used for agriculture by assessee or parent for 2 years before sale.
Agricultural land only 2 years agriculture usage required
