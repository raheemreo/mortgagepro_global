Key Rules & Conditions
🏠
Self-Occupied Property — ₹2 Lakh Cap
Maximum deduction of ₹2,00,000 per year on interest paid. Applicable only under Old Tax Regime. Applicable from year of possession.
Old Regime Only
🔑
Let-Out / Rented Property — No Limit
Full interest paid can be claimed as deduction against rental income. If loss occurs, up to ₹2L can be set off against other income; balance carried forward 8 years.
Unlimited Claim
🏗️
Under-Construction Property
Interest during construction period is aggregated and deducted in 5 equal instalments starting from year of completion/possession. Cap ₹30,000 if loan taken before 01-Apr-1999.
5-Year Spread
⚠️
New Tax Regime — Not Applicable
Section 24(b) deduction is NOT available if you opt for the New Tax Regime under Section 115BAC. Choose Old Regime to avail this benefit.
New Regime: No Benefit
