🛡️
LVR 35% Minimum Deposit
RBNZ requires residential property investors to have at least 35% deposit (LVR ≤65%). New builds may qualify at 20%. Check with your bank for current exemptions.
📝
Interest Deductibility Restored
From 1 April 2025: 100% of mortgage interest is deductible for rental properties again. This significantly improves after-tax returns vs 2023-2024 years.
🔒
Ring-Fencing Still Applies
Rental losses cannot offset your salary income. Losses are carried forward to offset future rental profits. Factor this into your cash flow planning.
🏠
Healthy Homes Compliance
All rental properties must meet Healthy Homes Standards (heating, insulation, ventilation, draught stopping, moisture/drainage). Compliance required by July 2025 for all tenancies.
