Key Exemptions
🏠
Main Home Exemption
Your primary residence (main home) is exempt from bright-line tax. Must be your main home for most of the ownership period. Mixed-use properties are partially taxable.
🏗️
New Builds (5yr Rule)
Under the 10yr era, new builds were subject to a shorter 5yr bright-line. From 1 Jul 2024, all properties are back to 2yr regardless of build type.
📅
Inherited Property
Property inherited from deceased estates is generally exempt from bright-line. The clock starts from when you received the inheritance if you later sell.
👨‍👩‍👧
Relationship Property
Transfers under the Property (Relationships) Act are generally exempt from bright-line. However, the receiving party inherits the original purchase date for future sales.
