NZ Yield Rules of Thumb
🎯
Gross Yield Targets (NZ)
5%+ gross = strong return · 4–5% = moderate · Below 4% = capital growth play. Net yield should be at least 3.5% to cover costs in high-rate environment.
🔢
The 1% Rule (Adapted NZ)
Weekly rent ÷ purchase price × 52 = gross yield. For a NZ$750K property, you need ~NZ$721/wk rent to achieve 5% gross yield.
📉
Net vs Gross Gap
Typical NZ deductions reduce gross yield by 1–2%. Rates (~$2,500–$5,000/yr), insurance (~$2,000–$3,000/yr), maintenance (~1% of value), property management (8% of rent).
⚠️
Interest Deductibility (2025)
From 1 Apr 2024: 80% of mortgage interest is deductible. From 1 Apr 2025: 100% deductible again for most properties. Brightline changes apply — check your property date.
