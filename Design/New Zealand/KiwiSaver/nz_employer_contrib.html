⚠️ ESCT Tax Alert
Employer Superannuation Contribution Tax (ESCT) is deducted from your employer's contribution before it reaches your KiwiSaver. This reduces your effective employer contribution.
📋 2025 Employer Contribution Rules (IRD)
  • Employer must contribute minimum 3% of gross salary
  • Employer contributions attract ESCT tax before deposit
  • ESCT rate based on employee's prior-year total remuneration + employer KiwiSaver
  • Government contributes 50 cents per $1 you contribute (max $521.43/yr)
  • To get max Govt contribution: contribute at least $1,042.86/yr
  • Employer cannot opt-out of contributing once employee is enrolled
  • Contributions go to your chosen KiwiSaver provider (or default)
