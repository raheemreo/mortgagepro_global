🇳🇿 How NZ Revolving Credit Works

1
Equity-Based Facility
Banks offer RC up to 80% LVR. E.g. $850K home, $420K mortgage → up to $260K RC available (subject to income).
2
Salary Goes In, Reduces Balance
Park your pay cheque into the RC — instantly reduces interest. Draw for bills/expenses as needed.
3
Interest Daily on Balance
ANZ Simplicity PLUS, ASB Orbit, Kiwibank Offset — all charge daily interest only on the outstanding drawn balance.
4
NZ Tax: No PIE, No Deductibility
RC interest on owner-occ is NOT tax deductible. For investment property, phased deductibility rules apply (2024 rules).
