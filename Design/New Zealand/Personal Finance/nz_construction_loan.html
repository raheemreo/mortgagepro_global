🇳🇿 NZ Construction Loan Process

1
Land Purchase → Slab (10–15%)
First drawdown covers land or initial groundwork/foundation. Interest-only on drawn balance. Bank sends QS for sign-off.
2
Frame Completed (20–25%)
Wall frames, trusses erected. Second drawdown released after inspection by bank's valuer or quantity surveyor.
3
Lock-Up (20–25%)
Roofing, cladding, windows fitted. Property is secure from weather. Bank issues third progress payment.
4
Fit-Out / Fixing (25–30%)
Internal linings, cabinetry, plumbing, electrical. Largest drawdown stage — budget carefully here.
5
Practical Completion (5–10%)
CCC (Code Compliance Certificate) obtained from council. Final drawdown. Loan converts to standard P&I mortgage.
