📌 Key Market Insights – March 2025
📉
Prices down ~18% from 2021 peak. National median fell from ~$925K (Nov 2021) to $785K (Mar 2025).
🔄
OCR cuts driving recovery. 1.75% of OCR cuts since Aug 2024 are supporting buyer confidence and lifting sales volumes.
📊
South Island outperforming. Christchurch (+1.8%) and Dunedin (+2.1%) leading national recovery vs. Auckland weakness.
🏗️
Listings rising. New listing inventory up ~12% YoY — giving buyers more choice and keeping prices subdued nationally.
