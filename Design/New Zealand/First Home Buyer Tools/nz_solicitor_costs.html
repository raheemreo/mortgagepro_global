What's Included
⚖️
Conveyancing Work
Title search, contract review, settlement prep, transfer registration. Typically NZ$1,500–$2,500 + GST. Complex leasehold properties can be higher.
📄
LIM Report (Council)
Land Information Memorandum from your local council. Shows zoning, consents, drainage, hazards. Cost: $250–$500. Urgent reports cost more.
🔍
Building Inspection
Independent structural & weathertightness report. Strongly recommended for all properties. Cost: $500–$900. Use a NZIBS-registered inspector.
🏦
Bank / Lender Docs
Solicitor acts on behalf of your lender to prepare mortgage security documents. Most solicitors charge $250–$400 for this.
🗺️
LINZ Title Registration
Land Information New Zealand. Title transfer and mortgage registration fees. Currently ~$100. FHB exempt from some charges.
Important Notes
⚠️
Leasehold & Auction Properties Cost More
Leasehold titles, cross-lease, and unit titles require additional title review. Auction purchases may need pre-auction legal checks ($300–$500 extra). Always budget a 15% contingency on top of estimates.
🥝
KiwiSaver Withdrawal Legal Costs
If using KiwiSaver for deposit, your solicitor will handle the withdrawal application. This is included in conveyancing fees at most firms. Allow $0–$200 extra.
💡
Shop Around — Get 3 Quotes
Conveyancing fees vary significantly. Request quotes from at least 3 NZ law firms. Tip: Law firms with dedicated property teams (e.g. Convex Legal, Hesketh Henry, Rainey Collins) often offer competitive flat fees for FHBs.
📅
When Are Fees Paid?
Most solicitor fees are invoiced at settlement. Building inspection and LIM fees are typically paid upfront. Budget these as out-of-pocket costs before settlement day.
