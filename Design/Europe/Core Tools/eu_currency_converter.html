🏛️ ECB & European Rate Guide
1
The ECB publishes reference rates daily at ~16:00 CET. These are non-commercial rates used as a benchmark.
2
EUR/CHF is capped by the Swiss National Bank's interventions, keeping the pair in a tight range near 0.95–0.97.
3
