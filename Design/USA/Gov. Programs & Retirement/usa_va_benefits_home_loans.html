VA Eligibility Requirements Service Periods
🎖️
Active Duty Service
90 days continuous active duty during wartime or 181 days during peacetime. Currently serving active duty members qualify after 90 days.
🏕️
National Guard / Reserves
6 years of service OR 90 days of active duty under Title 10 orders. Must have been honorably discharged or be currently serving.
💍
Surviving Spouses
Unremarried surviving spouse of veteran who died in service or from service-connected disability. Also eligible if veteran was POW/MIA.
📋
Certificate of Eligibility (COE)
Obtain via VA.gov eBenefits portal, lender can pull automatically, or mail VA Form 26-1880. Most lenders obtain same day.
🏠
Occupancy Requirement
Must intend to occupy the home as primary residence within 60 days of closing. Spouses may satisfy occupancy for deployed veterans.
