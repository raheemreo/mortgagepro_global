HUD Homebuyer Assistance
U.S. Dept. of Housing & Urban Development
Up to $25,000 Grant Income Limit: 120% AMI First-Time Only
The Downpayment Toward Equity Act (H.R. 4495) proposes $25,000 for first-generation buyers. Current state HUD programs provide $5,000–$15,000 in DPA. Must complete HUD-approved counseling (hud.gov). Income limits vary: 80%–120% AMI by metro area.

State DPA Programs
All 50 States + DC · Local HFAs
$2,500–$65,000 Forgivable or Deferred 620+ Credit Score
CA MyHome: deferred 3.5% DPA · TX TDHCA: up to 5% grant · FL Assist: $10,000 0% loan · NY SONYMA: $3,000 grant + low rate · GA Dream: $7,500 forgivable loan. Combined with FHA for lowest out-of-pocket.

Fannie Mae HomeReady®
3% Down · No PMI at 80% LTV · 2025
3% Down Payment Reduced PMI 620+ Score
Income limit 80% AMI. Allows non-occupant co-borrowers. Gift funds accepted for full down payment. Reduced MI (0.25% less than standard). Online homebuyer education required ($75 fee). Available for 1–4 unit primary residences.
🏠
Freddie Mac Home Possible®
3% Down · Low-to-Moderate Income · 2025
3% Down No Income Limit (Underserved Areas) 660+ Score
Income ≤ 80% AMI (standard), or no limit in underserved areas. Sweat equity counts toward down payment. Multiple co-borrowers allowed. MI cancels at 20% equity automatically. Comparable to HomeReady with slightly different qualifying criteria.
🏦
Good Neighbor Next Door
HUD · 50% Discount for Public Service Workers
50% Off List Price Teachers · Police · Firefighters 3-Year Residency Required
HUD sells foreclosed homes at 50% discount to eligible law enforcement, teachers (K-12), firefighters, and EMTs. Must live in the home for 36 months. Properties in HUD-designated revitalization areas. Listings rotate weekly on HUDhomestore.gov.
