Need-to-Know Facts NOAA/FEMA
⚠️
Hurricane deductibles are separate: Most Gulf/Atlantic states mandate a hurricane-specific deductible of 1–5% of Coverage A, not the standard flat deductible.
🌊
Flood ≠ Hurricane rider: Storm surge and inland flooding require a separate NFIP flood policy. Avg NFIP cost: $888/yr (2025).
🏠
FL Citizens Insurance: State insurer of last resort. 2025 avg $3,000–$6,000/yr wind-only. Over 1.2M FL policies.
🔨
FORTIFIED™ discount: IBHS FORTIFIED roof standard earns up to 25–30% off in FL and AL. Some insurers require it to write new policies.
📉
Hurricane Ian 2022: $60B+ insured loss in FL. Caused Farmers, Bankers Insurance, Lexington to exit the FL market 2023–2025.
