⏱️ 1031 Exchange Timeline (2025 IRS Rules)
1
Day 0
Close Relinquished Property
Sale of your existing investment property closes. Proceeds must go directly to a Qualified Intermediary (QI) — never touch the funds.
⚠️ Do NOT receive proceeds
2
Day 1–45
Identification Period
Identify up to 3 replacement properties (3-Property Rule) OR any number if total value ≤ 200% of relinquished (200% Rule). Must be in writing to QI.
⏰ Hard Deadline — No Extensions
3
Day 46–180
Exchange Period
Must close on identified replacement property within 180 days of the relinquished property closing OR the tax return due date (including extensions), whichever is earlier.
✅ 180-Day Window
4
Post-Close
Report on IRS Form 8824
File Form 8824 with your federal tax return. Report like-kind exchange, boot received, deferred gain, and new adjusted basis of replacement property.
📋 Form 8824 Required
