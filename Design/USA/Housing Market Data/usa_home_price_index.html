Market Context 2025 Analysis
📈
2025 HPI Growth: Cooling but Positive
After a scorching +20.6% YoY peak in 2022, HPI growth has cooled to +5.1% (FHFA) and +4.9% (Case-Shiller) in 2025. This is close to the historical norm of 3–5% annual appreciation.
🏙️
Northeast & Midwest Outperforming
New York, Chicago, and Boston lead with +7–8% YoY growth. The Sun Belt markets (Austin, Phoenix) that surged during COVID are now correcting, with Austin down −2.1% YoY.
🏠
Lock-In Effect Constraining Supply
With 78% of US mortgages locked in below 5%, existing homeowners are reluctant to sell, creating severe inventory shortages that continue to support prices despite high mortgage rates.
🔮
2025 Price Forecast (Consensus)
Fannie Mae (+4.8%), Freddie Mac (+3.0%), NAR (+2.0%), Goldman Sachs (+4.4%). Most analysts project continued modest appreciation in 2025, driven by supply constraints and resilient demand.
