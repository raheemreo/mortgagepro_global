Post-Closing Homeowner Tips
🏦
Use a High-Yield Savings Account
Top HYSAs (Marcus, Ally, SoFi) offer 4.5–5.0% APY in 2025. Your $36K earns ~$1,620/yr in interest while staying liquid.
4.5–5% APY
📊
Keep Fund Separate from Down Payment
Never co-mingle your emergency fund with your down payment savings. Lenders may flag funds used for closing as "depleted reserves."
Lender Tip
🔄
Replenish After Any Withdrawal
After using emergency funds (e.g., a roof leak or job loss), rebuild within 6–12 months. Set an automatic monthly transfer to stay on track.
Best Practice
📋
Lenders Want 2–6 Months Reserves
Many conventional and jumbo loan programs require proof of 2–6 months of PITI (Principal, Interest, Taxes, Insurance) in reserves after closing.
