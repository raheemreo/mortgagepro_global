FHA Minimum Property Standards (MPS) & Requirements (MPR)
Every FHA home must be safe, sound, and secure
A specially trained FHA appraiser checks the home twice: once for market value, once against HUD's habitability checklist. Fail it, and repairs are required before closing.

What Gets a Pass vs. a Repair Order
✅ Usually Forgiven (Cosmetic)
• Worn carpet/flooring
• Outdated finishes
• Small drywall holes
• Faded paint (post-1978)
• Minor cabinet wear
❌ Requires Repair First
• Exposed/frayed wiring
• Active roof leaks
• Broken windows
• No working heat source
• Peeling paint (pre-1978)
