Key Insights
🏛️
502 Direct: Deepest Subsidy
For households at 50–80% of Area Median Income (AMI). Payment subsidy can reduce your effective rate to as low as 1%. Must apply directly through USDA Rural Development office — not through a bank.
🏦
502 Guaranteed: More Accessible
For households up to 115% AMI. Apply through any of 1,800+ approved lenders nationwide. Faster processing (30–45 days) and no loan limit makes it the choice for 90% of USDA borrowers.
⚠️
Recapture Rule — Direct Only
If you sell or refinance a 502 Direct loan within 9 years, USDA may "recapture" some of the subsidy received. Plan to stay or ask USDA about the recapture calculation before selling.
