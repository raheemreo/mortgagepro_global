✅ What USDA Streamline Covers
💰
No out-of-pocket costs: Closing costs and the 1% upfront guarantee fee can be financed into the new loan — no money needed at closing.
⚡
Faster closing: Since no appraisal, credit check, or income verification is needed, streamline refis typically close in 20–30 days.
📉
Two types: USDA Streamline-Assist (most popular, no payment history review) and USDA Streamline (standard, requires 12 on-time payments).
🔄
Reset term: Refinancing resets your loan to a new 30-year term. Run the numbers to ensure total interest savings outweigh the term extension.
⚠️ Things to Watch For
📅
Term reset: A new 30-year term means you may pay more total interest even with a lower rate — especially if you've already paid 5+ years on the existing loan.
💸
Financed costs: Rolling closing costs into the loan means you're paying interest on those costs for 30 years — factor this into your break-even analysis.
🏡
Stay threshold: If you plan to sell within 2–3 years, refinancing costs may exceed savings. Only makes sense if you stay past break-even.
