📋 USDA Property Eligibility Rules (2025)
🏙️
Population Limit: Property must be in an area with a population under 35,000 (some exceptions for areas up to 50,000 if transitioning).
🗺️
Official Tool: Always verify at eligibility.sc.egov.usda.gov — the USDA updates boundaries periodically.
🏡
Property Types: Single-family homes, condos (USDA-approved), PUDs, and new construction all qualify if in eligible areas.
⚠️
Waiver Possible: Some areas exceeding limits may still qualify if they lack sufficient mortgage credit — request a waiver through your lender.
📅
2020 Census Data: USDA now uses 2020 Census figures. Some previously eligible areas may no longer qualify. Re-verify any prior checks.
