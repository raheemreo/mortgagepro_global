State Licensing Snapshot
🐻
CaliforniaLicense Req.
CSLB state license · $25,000 contractor bond · 4 yrs verified experience · ~$650 initial fees
⭐
TexasNo State Lic.
No statewide GC license — local permits & registration apply (Houston, Dallas, Austin, San Antonio)
🚗
MichiganLicense Req.
Residential Builder license (LARA) · required for jobs ≥$600 · 60-hr prelicensure course + exam
🦞
MassachusettsLicense Req.
Construction Supervisor License · 3 yrs verified experience · general liability insurance required
⛰️
Colorado / Kansas / WyomingNo State Lic.
No statewide general contractor license — local city/county permitting governs instead
🎰
NevadaLicense Req.
Licensed through the State Contractors Board · experience and qualifying-individual rules apply
Licensing varies by state and changes often — verify current rules with your state contractor licensing board.
