Document Checklist
W-2 Forms (Last 2 Years)
From all employers. Must include employer name, EIN, wages & federal withholding.
Required
Federal Tax Returns (1040, 2 Years)
All pages, all schedules. Signed & dated. IRS transcripts may also be required via 4506-C.
Required
30-Day Pay Stubs (Most Recent)
Year-to-date earnings must match W-2 trajectory. Some lenders require 60 days.
Required
Employer Verification Letter (VOE)
Confirmation of employment, position, salary, start date on employer letterhead.
Required
Bonus / Commission History (2 Years)
Required if variable income used in qualification. Must average over 24 months.
If Applicable
IRS 4506-C Form (Signed)
Authorization for lender to obtain tax transcripts directly from IRS.
Required
Bank Statements (2–3 Months)
All pages, all accounts. Large deposits ≥$5,000 must be documented with source letters.
Required
Investment / Brokerage Statements
Most recent 3 months. Stocks, bonds, mutual funds. 70% of value counted for liquid reserves.
Required
Retirement Account Statements (401k/IRA)
Most recent quarter. 60–70% of vested balance counted if under 59½ due to early withdrawal penalty.
If Applicable
Gift Letter (if applicable)
For jumbo loans, gifts typically must be 100% from immediate family. Letter must state no repayment expected.
If Down Payment Gift
Government-Issued Photo ID
Driver's license, passport, or state ID. Must be current and not expired. Both sides required.
Required
Social Security Number / Card
Used for credit pull authorization. Most lenders accept last 4 digits after verification.
Required
Credit Report Explanation Letters
Written explanation for any derogatory marks, late payments, or collections in last 7 years.
If Applicable
Signed Purchase Agreement
Fully executed contract with all addendums, seller disclosures, and contingency waivers.
Required
Property Appraisal (Ordered by Lender)
Jumbo loans often require 2 appraisals for loans over $2M. USPAP-compliant, full interior inspection.
Required
Title Report / Preliminary Title Search
Confirm property is free of liens, judgments, or encumbrances. Title insurance required at closing.
Required
Homeowner's Insurance Binder
Coverage must equal replacement cost or loan amount, whichever is less. Named mortgagee clause required.
Required
Flood Insurance (FEMA Zone)
Mandatory if property in FEMA Special Flood Hazard Area (Zone A or V). Min. coverage = loan amount or max available.

💡 Speed Up Your Jumbo Approval
Jumbo underwriters manually review every file. Organize documents digitally in labeled folders (Income, Assets, Property) before uploading. Pre-write explanation letters for any credit inquiries or large deposits. Having 18 months of PITI in liquid reserves — not just 12 — can fast-track approval at most top-tier lenders.
