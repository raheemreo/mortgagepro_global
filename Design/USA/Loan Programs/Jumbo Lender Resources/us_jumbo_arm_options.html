✅ Who Benefits Most from a Jumbo ARM?
🏡
Short-Term Owners: Planning to sell or move within 5–10 years. You leave before rates adjust.
💼
High-Income Earners: Income growth outpaces potential rate increases. Payment increase is manageable.
📈
Rate-Drop Believers: Expect Fed to cut rates before your fixed period ends. ARM starts lower and may fall further.
🔄
Refi-Savvy Borrowers: Plan to refinance into a fixed rate if rates drop before adjustment kicks in.
⚠️
Caution: If rates rise to cap, a $1.2M ARM can cost $3,700+/mo more than today. Ensure reserves cover worst-case scenario.
