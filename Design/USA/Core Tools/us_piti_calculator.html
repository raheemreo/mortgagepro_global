💡 PITI Pro Tip
Lenders use PITI to qualify you. Most conventional lenders cap front-end DTI at 28% and back-end at 36–43%. FHA allows up to 31%/43%. A 20% down payment eliminates PMI, saving $100–$300/mo on a typical loan.
