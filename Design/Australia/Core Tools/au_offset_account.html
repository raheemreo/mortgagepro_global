Offset Tips
🏦 Offset Account Guide (Australia)
1
Every dollar in your offset reduces the loan balance used to calculate interest — effectively earning your mortgage rate tax-free.
2
In 2026, best HISA rates sit around 5.00%. Your mortgage at 6.09% means offset beats savings by ~1.09% (pre-tax equivalent: ~1.56% for a 30% taxpayer).
3
Park your salary in the offset before paying bills — even a few days' difference saves measurable interest over time.
4
100% offset accounts (most common in Australia) give full dollar-for-dollar reduction. Partial offset accounts only reduce a portion — read the fine print.
